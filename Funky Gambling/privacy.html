Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information Funky Gambling stores and how it is used. By using the bot, you agree to this policy.

2. Data we store

The bot stores only the minimum data required for its features to work, for example:

  • Your Discord user ID for levels, balances and cooldowns.
  • Per-server settings such as chosen channels, toggles and filter configuration.
  • Aggregated statistics such as how many tokens you have or your current level.

No message content is permanently stored just for logging purposes. Messages may be briefly processed in memory to detect insults or run commands, then discarded.

3. What we do not store

  • We do not log or archive full chat histories.
  • We do not store passwords or sensitive personal information.
  • We do not sell or share data with third parties.

4. Third-party services

Funky Gambling runs entirely on Discord's platform and may rely on hosting providers or databases. These services may have access to the data required to run the bot (for example, server IDs and user IDs), but they are not allowed to use it for their own purposes beyond providing the service.

5. Data removal

If you wish to remove data for your server, you can:

  • Remove the bot from your server.
  • Contact the bot owner via Discord and request deletion of stored data for your server or user ID.

6. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the bot after changes means you accept the updated policy.

7. Contact

For questions about privacy or data, please reach out to the bot owner via Discord.
